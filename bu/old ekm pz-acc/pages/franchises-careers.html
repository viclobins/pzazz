Franchises & Careers

P-zazz is the UK’s award-winning, market-leading brand in primary school-based performing and creative arts clubs, founded and led by Esther Boughton, a finalist in Hertfordshire’s ‘Entrepreneur of the Year’ awards.

Expanding rapidly across the country from Hertfordshire roots, P-zazz leaders and franchisees run clubs that inspire, engage and educate children and increasingly, clients in the care home community.

At P-zazz our quest for excellence extends beyond performing and creative classes to the franchisees we welcome and the way we do business. Professionalism and attention to detail are at the heart of P-zazz so we understand that making the right choice for your future starts with understanding the opportunity!


 

Why a franchise with P-zazz?

You’ll want to be part of a successful team, but you’ll also want genuine support – real help, when you need it

P-zazz is exceptional in offering in-house expertise and skills in all areas. Our support and guidance will match your enthusiasm and drive – every step of the way. Plus you’ll benefit from award-winning systems. Our custom-written cloud-based database “Zing” (Finalist Innovation in Technology class, Inspiring Herts Awards 2012) streamlines administration, maximising time to develop your business.

You’ll understand that reputation and trust take time to build. Brand strength is crucial!

We’re incredibly proud to say that P-zazz has become synonymous with excellence and quality – bringing tangible benefits to all of our franchisees. Being successful in school and care environments means gaining the trust and respect of many. This achievement has allowed us to grow beyond expectations – offering a real competitive advantage to franchisees taking the P-zazz brand to fresh markets.

You’ll want to avoid taking risks (so do we)!

Becoming a franchisee is a big decision. Here at P-zazz we have worked with the BFA and financial experts to test and perfect our business model. You’ll be investing in a carefully structured and realistic plan – the only thing missing is you!

With a relatively small initial outlay and a short ‘up and running’ lead time, it’s drive and initiative which determine just how soon you’ll reap the financial and personal rewards P-zazz franchisees enjoy.

You’ll want to know your business has unlimited potential

Working with primary schools and wider communities we offer an extensive range of classes and services. From lunchtimes and after school to Weekend Clubs, Breakfast Clubs, Inset Day Cover, Holiday Clubs, P-zazz Parties and Gold (care home) Sessions – you’ll be choosing an endless business opportunity with scope for you to really fly.

You’ll want to do things your way

Passionate about teaching performing and creative arts? Recognize P-zazz as the best franchise opportunity and anticipate managing a team of leaders? Want term time only or looking for an all year round business? Whatever your business approach, the P-zazz model works; please take a look at our testimonials and ask for more information.
