Starting a P-zazz club at your school

We are very experienced at starting clubs in primary schools and make it as easy as possible. Recognising that schools are busy places, we do everything we can to make the introduction of a P-zazz club a pleasure.

Join in the Experience: Taster Assembly

We offer schools a free Taster Assembly, where one or more of our Leaders come into an assembly for about 10 minutes to give the children a taste of what being in a P-zazz club is like. The children love the opportunity to meet the Leader and to take part in the activities.

No Hassle: A Choice of Payment / Administration Options

There is a choice of 4 options to make our clubs accessible for schools in all demographic areas. Please click here for more information - we are always happy to work with you to make our clubs accessible for as many of your children as possible.

Fair Access: 2 P'zazz Funded Places

In clubs where the parents pay directly and we do all of the administration, we will offer 2 free places for each club we run. We ask you to allocate those places and we'll do the rest.
