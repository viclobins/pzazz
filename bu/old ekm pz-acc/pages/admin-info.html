Administration Information

P-zazz was started in primary schools and the whole model has been created with the needs of schools at the forefront. We are proud of our reputation for offering great clubs which are superbly administered. We recognise how busy school offices are so have tailored everything we do to maximise the service we give school staff.

Our Leaders

  • have Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) / Enhanced Criminal Record Bureau (CRB) checks

  • are subject to rigorous recruitment procedures

  • receive training and ongoing supervision

  • the vast majority of them are first-aid trained: 100% will be by July 2014.

Insurance

  • Our certificate of Public Liability & Employer's Liability Insurance is sent to schools when clubs begin and annually thereafter.

Head Office

  • Our Head Office is manned by friendly staff who are trained to give an excellent level of service to schools and parents.
  • Office hours during term-time are 9.15am - 4.00pm, Monday - Friday.

Club Administration

  • We felt so strongly about getting the administration of the clubs right every time that we commissioned our own database.
  • This allows us to ensure that schools where our clubs are run receive up-to-the-minute information about the children enrolled.
  • We can therefore administer our clubs to a very high standard, second to none.
  • We were delighted that our database was nominated for the acclaimed Inspiring Herts Innovation in Technology Award 2012.
