About Us

Since 2005, primary schools around the UK have been choosing P-zazz to provide the most inspirational and exciting range of creative clubs.

The enjoyment and development of each child are at the heart of everything we do. We will work with you to tailor and deliver a range of activities to build confidence and self-esteem - with fun guaranteed!

We run clubs that inspire, engage and educate children and increasingly, clients in the care home community.

Our approach goes hand-in-hand with our famously professional service. P-zazz offers flexible options with regards to club choice, costs and administration – ensuring the clubs are run in the way that best suits our schools and families.

Every club welcomes every child. We are always happy to discuss any particular needs and work hard to remove any obstacles to joining in the fun!

Established in 2005, we've run over 1000 inspirational clubs across the UK and in Ireland with 15,000+ participants, with new groups being opened all the time.

Our primary school & nursery range of lunchtime and after-school clubs includes:

  • Sing
  • Act
  • Perform
  • Dance
  • Create

Our engaging sessions are run by performing and creative arts experts. Proud of our reputation for offering great clubs superbly administered, for children we also run fabulous parties, weekend clubs, holiday clubs, inset day cover and PPA cover.

Additionally, P-zazz Gold Sessions bring specially trained, music professionals into residential and care homes, providing top quality live music and song.

We pride ourselves on bringing the pleasure and therapeutic power of music to elderly people not just as entertainment but as reminiscence therapy and as a way to combat dementia, Alzheimer’s and other challenges of old age.

Everyone dreams of having a full and active old age, in a safe, joyful environment. Almost five years of work within residential and care homes have given P-zazz a genuine understanding of the challenges facing professionals trying daily to fulfil these dreams.

History

P-zazz was started by Esther Boughton, Managing Director, in 2005. She put her experience of directing choirs to the test in her local primary school, devising an exciting way of singing which energised the children, giving them passion for music. She began by singing in the classroom and launched P-zazz as a singing club for the rest of the school the following term. Over 70 children signed up (25% of the school) which firmly answered the question of whether there was demand!

In 2008, Esther took P-zazz to other schools and the style of club was so well received that she started to recruit P-zazz Leaders, training & supporting them to deliver excellent sessions across the UK. As it grew, the company diversified to build the portfolio of clubs it now offers, always focussing on the expertise of the Leaders and their ability to communicate those skills in a way which the group members love.

The successful growth of P-zazz has been due also to Esther's background in business. Her 12 years as a Bank Manager have influenced the way the company has developed and prestigious nominations have followed as a result.
