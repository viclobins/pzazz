About P-Zazz

P-zazz is the UK’s, leading provider of primary school-based Performing & Creative Arts Clubs.
Since 2005, we’ve run over 1,000 clubs with a staggering 15,000 participants!

We believe all of us are happiest doing something we love, being listened to and understood.
This principle underpins the way we run every class and club.

P-zazz clubs inspire, engage and educate children and increasingly, clients in the care home community, offering all participants new skills and experiences - in an inclusive, supportive environment.
