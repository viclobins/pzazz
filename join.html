Be a P-zazz Leader

Do you have extraordinary creative or performance skills? Want to work with young people? Our team of wonderful Leaders needs you!

What is a leader?

Leaders are the talented professionals who run P-zazz clubs and sessions, in schools and care environments.

We’re looking for people who share our commitment to passing on a love of performing and the arts. A great P-zazz Leader inspires every participant to discover and embrace music, performance or art – setting up a lifelong love!

Perfect job, perfect package!

  • great pay & vast opportunities
  • rewarding & varied work
  • professional training & support
  • flexible hours & convenient locations

How do I become a leader?

You’ll need to demonstrate your musical or performing abilities, have experience working with young people and be an excellent communicator!

Our comprehensive training course will test your skills and equip you with the knowledge and confidence to represent P-zazz. This includes first aid training and IT skills.

We’ll take care of your DBS check and once this is all in place we’ll supply materials, lesson plans, uniform and organise your hours. You could be up and running in a few weeks!
