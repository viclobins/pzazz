What is a P-zazz Gold Session?

These very special one hour sessions bring our specially trained, music and performance professionals into care and support environments.

P-zazz Gold delivers the pleasure and therapeutic powers of music, art and drama to people with a broad range of needs and abilities, regardless of age.

Our objective is to make each session entertaining, interactive and rewarding, for every participant.

Why Choose Us?

Over five years of work with residential and care groups, has given P-zazz a genuine understanding of the challenges faced.

We‘ll work with you and your group - whatever their mental, emotional or physical needs - supporting your work and complementing the effectiveness of your overall service.

All P-zazz Leaders are DBS vetted - for your peace of mind.

We DO EVERYTHING!

There’s no need for anything to be provided.

Our skilled P-zazz Leader will play their own instrument, your piano (if you have one) or use a backing-track Equally, for a creative session – we’ll consult with you and provide all materials.
